# Product Guide: Tiny Invoicing

## Initial Concept
This application is a "Tiny Invoicing" system, designed for managing invoices and client billing.

## Core Purpose
The primary purpose of this application is to provide a lightweight system for handling invoicing and client billing operations. It aims to simplify the process of creating, managing, and tracking invoices for small businesses or individual users.

## Features

### Invoice Management
- **Create Invoices:** Easily create new invoices with client details and multiple line items.
- **Automatic Calculations:** Real-time calculation of line item totals and overall invoice grand total.
- **View Details:** Comprehensive read-only view of specific invoices, including all itemized charges.
- **List and Manage:** A centralized view to list all invoices with support for pagination and sorting.
